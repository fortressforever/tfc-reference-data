### Damage
All damage data was measured with the rocket exploding at the player's origin exactly.

#### Damage to an enemy
* Minimum: 83
* Maximum: 101
* Average: ~92

#### Self-damage
* Minimum: 53
* Maximum: 71
* Average: ~62
